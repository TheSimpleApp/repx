# RepX MVP - Executive Summary

## 🎯 **Product Overview**

RepX is a mobile-first gamification platform that transforms new sales rep onboarding from a 60% retention rate to 89% by systematizing contact mining and structuring the critical first 28 days with clear tasks, immediate rewards, and measurable progress tracking.

---

## 💰 **Business Opportunity**

### **Market Problem**
- **$15B annually** spent on recruiting platforms (Indeed, LinkedIn, agencies)
- **60% first-year turnover** costs companies $15,000+ per failed rep
- **2M+ field sales reps** need better onboarding systems
- **Personal networks underutilized** - only 25% of contacts ever approached systematically

### **RepX Solution**
- **Eliminates external recruiting costs** by leveraging reps' personal phone contacts
- **Systematic contact mining** through dating app-style swipe interface
- **Structured 28-day program** with immediate cash rewards for task completion
- **89% retention rate** through proven onboarding methodology

### **Revenue Model**
- **SaaS Pricing**: $99-199 per rep per month
- **Target Market**: 10,000 D2D companies, 500K reps
- **5-Year Projection**: $60M ARR with 33,500 active reps
- **LTV:CAC Ratio**: 35:1 with 85% gross margins

---

## 🎮 **Core Platform Features**

### **1. Contact Mining System**
- **Phone Contact Import**: Sync contacts from device (phone only for MVP)
- **Swipe Categorization**: Left (skip), Right (select customer/recruit), Super swipe up (star)
- **3-Tier Reward System**: Different cash bonuses based on contact quality assessment
- **Immediate Gratification**: Instant cash rewards for contact processing

### **2. 28-Day Onboarding Program**
- **Weeks 1-2**: Control-based tasks (knock doors, complete training, attend meetings)
- **Weeks 3-4**: Outcome-based tasks (set demos, get referrals, earn reviews)
- **Visual Progress Tracking**: Real-time dashboard showing advancement
- **Cash Rewards**: Direct payments for task completion (training pay model)

### **3. Mobile-First Experience**
- **Native iOS/Android Apps**: Built with FlutterFlow for rapid development
- **Offline Capability**: Works without internet in field environments
- **GPS Verification**: Automatic verification of door knocking and field activities
- **Push Notifications**: Task reminders and achievement celebrations

## 🏗️ **Technical Architecture**

### **Frontend Stack**
- **FlutterFlow**: Rapid mobile app development platform
- **Flutter/Dart**: Cross-platform native mobile applications
- **Firebase**: Authentication, database, storage, push notifications
- **Offline-First**: Full functionality without internet connection

### **Backend Stack**
- **Firebase Firestore**: NoSQL database for scalability and real-time updates
- **Cloud Functions**: Serverless backend processing
- **Stripe Integration**: Secure payment processing for cash rewards
- **Third-Party APIs**: SMS (Twilio), Email (SendGrid), Analytics

### **Database Options**
- **Option A**: Firebase Firestore (NoSQL) - Recommended for FlutterFlow
- **Option B**: Supabase PostgreSQL - Alternative with SQL benefits
- **Both options fully documented** with complete schemas and security rules

## 📱 **User Experience Design**

### **Core User Flow**
1. **Day 1**: Import phone contacts → Learn swipe interface → Process first 50 contacts → Earn first rewards
2. **Week 1-2**: Complete control-based tasks → Earn cash bonuses → Build foundation habits
3. **Week 3-4**: Achieve outcome-based results → Higher rewards → Generate business value
4. **Ongoing**: Systematic contact follow-up → Customer/recruit development → Sustained success

### **Swipe Interface (Like Dating App)**
- **Left Swipe**: Skip contact (no reward)
- **Right Swipe**: Select as customer or recruit prospect → Choose tier → Earn cash reward
- **Super Swipe Up**: Star as high-priority → Higher reward tier
- **Progress Bar**: Visual progress through contact list
- **Immediate Feedback**: Cash reward notification after each categorization

### **Design Principles**
- **Pure Flat Design**: No borders, shadows, or gradients
- **Mobile-Optimized**: Thumb-friendly, one-handed usage
- **Gamification**: Progress bars, achievement celebrations, reward animations
- **Offline-First**: Works in field environments without connectivity
